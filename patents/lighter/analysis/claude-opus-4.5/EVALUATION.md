# Evaluation: Zippo Pocket Lighter (US Patent 2,032,695)

## Patent Information
- **Patent Number:** US 2,032,695
- **Title:** Pocket Lighter
- **Inventors:** George G. Gimera and George G. Blaisdell
- **Date:** March 3, 1936
- **Analysis Model:** Claude Opus 4.5

## Overall Assessment

### Strengths
1. **Core innovation captured** - The "no external latches" design principle well understood
2. **Latch mechanism explained** - The clever lever/plunger system accurately described
3. **Complete part identification** - All numbered parts from patent correctly identified
4. **Practical benefits clear** - Fuel capacity, durability, and ease of use advantages noted

### Areas for Improvement
1. **3D model detail** - Internal latch mechanism deserves detailed modeling
2. **Wind screen perforations** - Specific hole pattern from patent could be shown
3. **Assembly sequence** - How parts fit together could be animated
4. **Material specifications** - Sheet metal construction details not fully explored
